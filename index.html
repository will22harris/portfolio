About Me

I'm a senior at UT Austin majoring in Advertising with a Sports Media minor and an Elements of Business certificate. I'm interested in the space where sports, culture, and storytelling collide — and I'm actively building toward a career that lives there.

My work spans branded content, social media production, conceptual advertising, and photography. Outside the classroom, I serve as VP & Media Director of DA&PR, a student-run advertising and PR organization focused on diversity in the industry.

I'm based in Austin, TX — a city I genuinely love and plan to stay rooted in as its sports, media, and advertising ecosystem keeps growing.

04 / Project · Client Work

Social AdsApparelAustin, TX

As the CEO's Creative & Operations Assistant at Dumb Love — an Austin-born apparel brand built around the "casually athletic, socially competitive" ethos — I produced social media ads to support new product drops alongside broader brand growth and retail expansion work. 05 / Project

Velveeta Campaign

PrintCopywritingSatire

A series of fake-headline print ads for Velveeta in the style of The Onion — using deadpan, news-anchor voice and absurd premises to reframe processed cheese as the answer to humanity's biggest problems. The work leans into satire as a brand vehicle: surreal enough to stop the scroll, dry enough to feel editorial.

06 / Project · Texas Creative Showcase

Eau de Comet

ConceptArt DirectionPrint

A luxury fragrance spoof built around Comet bleach — high-fashion editorial styling crashed into the most aggressively unsexy product on the cleaning aisle. The campaign sells "where clean meets seductive," borrowing the visual language of Dior and Tom Ford to dress up an industrial cleanser as a designer scent. Featured at the Texas Creative end-of-year showcase.
